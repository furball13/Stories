Title: "The Time Capsule"

Prompt: In a small town, residents unearth a mysterious time capsule buried decades ago. Inside, they find a series of letters addressed to each of them, detailing significant events in their future lives. As they grapple with the shocking revelations, the town begins to change in unexpected ways, challenging their beliefs about fate and free will. Explore the impact of foreknowledge on their choices and relationships, and reveal the true origin of the time capsule, leaving readers questioning the nature of destiny.

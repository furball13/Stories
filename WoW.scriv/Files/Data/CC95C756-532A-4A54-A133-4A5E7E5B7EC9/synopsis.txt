Carolyn and Kamery follow a suspicious man in Menethil Harbor; he initially eludes them, then he and his friends jump Carolyn when the sisters split up to look for him. Elthas, having just arrived on the ship from Auberdine, charges in to save her. Their next lead is to Theramore. Jaina Proudmoore brings Bryna along to aid adventurers in confronting the suspicious Private Hendel, and the adventurers turn out to be her friends.
